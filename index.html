About

Vision

Our department is dedicated to advancing as a globally recognized academic institution known for pioneering research and a robust knowledge base. We aim to be a leading contributor to the scientific community in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, aligning our growth with global standards to address industry and societal needs.

Mission

To empower human resources with cutting-edge knowledge in materials science, addressing global challenges in resource efficiency, fostering expertise through education, and driving impactful research and industry solutions.

History

Founded in 1965, the Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at VNIT Nagpur has evolved into a leading center for metallurgical education and research. With B.Tech., M.Tech., and Ph.D. programs, the department is dedicated to advancing materials science, industry collaboration, and cutting-edge research in fields like materials processing and failure analysis.
