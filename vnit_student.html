Student Placement Process

The placement process for students at VNIT Nagpur is designed to ensure effective career placements through a structured and supportive approach. The key steps include registering on the dedicated placement portal to initiate the process and paying the placement assistance fee to access comprehensive support services. Students are required to participate in pre-placement training sessions organized by the VNIT Placement Cell. However, those who have previously completed similar training programs may proceed directly to the pre-placement task assessments. Only students meeting the minimum cut-off score in these assessments can move forward to explore job opportunities, submit applications, and track their application statuses through the portal. The process also involves understanding the selection criteria and processes outlined by recruiters and carefully evaluating job offers received. By adhering to these steps, VNIT students can navigate the placement process effectively and improve their chances of securing rewarding employment opportunities.
